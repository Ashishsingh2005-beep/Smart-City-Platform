Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to resolve a complaint?

Resolution times vary by priority. High priority safety issues are typically addressed within 24 hours. Routine maintenance may take 3-5 days.

Can I report anonymously?

Yes, but creating an account allows you to track status updates and get notified when the issue is resolved.

How does the AI prioritization work?

Our smart AI analyzes keywords in your description to identify emergencies (like 'fire', 'accident') and automatically assigns a high priority.
